KidSize Soccer Competition of RoboCup Humanoid League

Oct 1, 2020

I was a former member of ZJUDancer, a robot team in Zhejiang University for KidSize Soccer Competition of RoboCup Humanoid League. I worked with my teamates to develop motion planning software for soccer robot using ROS and C++. We experimented our software with simulation using Gazebo. In 2017 and 2019, we achieved KidSize Soccer Competition 2nd Place in RoboCup KidSize Humanoid League.
